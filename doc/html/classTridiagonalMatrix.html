Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class TridiagonalMatrix< T >

TridiagonalMatrix Tridiagonal matrix storage for compression. The matrix has the form:

\[ \begin{pmatrix} a_1 & b_1 & 0 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ c_2 & a_2 & b_2 & 0 & \dots & 0 \\ 0 & c_3 & a_3 & b_3 & \ddots & 0 \\ \vdots & \ddots & \ddots & \ddots & \ddots & 0 \\ 0 & \ddots & 0 & c_{n-1} & a_{n - 1} & b_{n-1} \\ 0 & \dots & 0 & 0 & c_n & a_n \\ \end{pmatrix} \]

But only the diagonals are stored to simplify computation and reduce the memory usage. It is possible to store every numerical value supports all mathematical field operations.
